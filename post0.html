Meditation and Concentration

Meditation is a surgical instrument that cuts off from you everything that is not yours, and leaves only what is your true being. If you do not use ordinary life as a meditation method, your meditation will become a kind of escape from reality.

The essence of concentration while meditating is to make consciousness one-pointed, strong, i.e. learn to control your attention. For this, an object is selected and you are engaged in sedentary practice, in which you concentrate your attention on the object (deity, contemplation of breath, sound, light, various objects.) The object is the entry point and we get into states that are not connected with the object
