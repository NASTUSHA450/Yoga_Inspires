Health and General Perception

Equilibrium within each is maintained by the balance of the physical , mental and spiritual spheres . Ask yourself a question: what am I doing to maintain my physical body? How do I work with my psyche? What am I doing for my spiritual development? If you only occupy yourself with the physical body, and spend some time on psychosomatics and do not think about the spiritual components of life. There is no balance in one (in particular in the spiritual sphere) - this leads to an imbalance in the other two areas. The harmonious development of the three spheres simultaneously leads to full health .

Physical body

- Food: water at least 2 liters, and even better 3 liters per day; food that does not comfort and acidify the body; sufficient time between meals.

- Conditions favorable for the physical body: sun, clean air, nature.

- Working with the body: exercises for strengthening, relaxation, recovery.

- Rest for the body: sleep, dreamless, thoughtless.

Mental body

- Feeding the Mind with Positive Objects: Mind needs more than survival itself. Music, art, positive emotions, meditation are spiritual food. My little happiness is bound to be lost. Do not hide from people, support each other, think nobly, great aspirations for everyone.

- Conditions that are favorable for the mental body: places of power, these are places for meditation, where the soul feels calm, harmony.

- Rest for the mind is also a dream. There is a strong conflict in the mind, stop physically and try to stop thoughts or slow down - this is the transition to meditation. Chanting mantras or prayers will even automatically have a beneficial effect on the mind. Also, the practice of silence allows you to accumulate energy and when you say a word, it becomes capacious and weighty, it has meaning and energy. Emotion sits in you, tired of taking apart, deciding, analyzing. Stop and observe, rest, look from the side.

- Purification: discharge of emotions through laughter, tears, dance, music, through words, possibly poetry. Resolve conflicts with family, friends, passers-by on the street, which leaves a negative aftertaste on the soul. To clear the conscience (to remove the burden from the soul - and so they say) - to admit it and repent. Take austerity for correction. VERY DIFFICULT!!! Finish all imperfections. The past was and it is not necessary to drag it along like a train all my life.
