About me:

Hello, I'm Tatiana!

My qualifications :

I have graduated the medical University of Russia as a pharmacist, laboratory assistant and phytotherapist and worked in the Republican Clinical Hospital as a laboratory assistant.

My yoga practice :

In Russia I got acquainted with the Aengara yoga, in Cyprus with hatha and therapeutic yoga. Trained in southern India and practiced hatha yoga with one of the leading teachers of Pranam Yoga Joseph (Sivavanda system and BKS Iyengar). For the last 7 years I have been living in Pafos, Cyprus and all these years I have been closely engaged in my physical health and yoga practice.

Today yoga is my life :

At the very first lesson, the master said that I was a ready yoga teacher, this inspired me to start sharing my experience with others. Yoga is an opportunity to work every day with my physical body, emotions, thoughts and develop spiritually. I pay great attention to breathing and meditation. Communication and work in a group is important for me, this gives everyone the opportunity to learn more about their inner-selfs.
